# Contributors

AMASAMYA exists because of the people listed here. Every entry below
represents a real test session, a code contribution, or a piece of
feedback that changed the tool.

If you contributed and your name should be on this list, please reply
to your contact email or open an issue — I'll add you within 48 hours.

## Beta testers

People who gave time and honest feedback during the closed beta.
Listed in the order their feedback arrived. Public credit is opt-in;
testers who chose to remain anonymous are listed as "Beta Tester"
followed by their session number.

## Acknowledgements

AMASAMYA depends on or learns from the following projects and standards:

- **W3C WAI** — for WCAG 2.2 and the WAI-ARIA specifications.
- **Deque axe-core** — used in this project's own self-audit pipeline.
- **PDF.js** (Mozilla) — powers the tagged-PDF structure walker.
- **DAISY Consortium** — for the EPUB Accessibility 1.1 specification
  that informs the EPUB engine's checks.
- **WebAIM** — for two decades of public accessibility documentation
  that, frankly, taught the entire field.

## Credit policy

- We credit by name only with explicit opt-in (Q6.1 on the feedback
  form, or via direct reply to your tester email).
- We never publish email addresses.
- Anonymity is the default. Saying nothing means you don't get listed.
- Optional links may point to LinkedIn, a personal site, a Mastodon
  profile, a GitHub profile — anywhere you publicly represent yourself.
  We will not accept links to commercial product pages or marketing
  sites without prior conversation.
- If you change your mind later — wanting to be added, removed, or
  renamed — reply to the email that brought you here and we'll update
  the list within 48 hours.
