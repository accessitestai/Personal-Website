About Me

I am an Accessibility Architect and Digital Inclusion Strategist based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. With over 16 years of experience, I define and deliver enterprise-grade accessibility programmes across web, mobile, and document platforms.

As a blind professional who lost sight at age 12, I navigate the digital world daily using JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver, and a Braille display. This gives me lived, first-hand insight that no sighted consultant can replicate — making me uniquely qualified to evaluate, architect, and champion truly inclusive digital experiences.

I have a proven record leading large-scale accessibility transformations for global clients including Google, Bank of Montreal, and Dun & Bradstreet. I build institutional capability — not just audit reports.
