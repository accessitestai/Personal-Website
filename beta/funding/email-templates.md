# AMASAMYA Funding Outreach - Email Templates

Five templates, one per funding track. **Customise every bracketed
field before sending.** Plain text. Under 200 words each. Lead with
specifics, not aspiration.

---

## Template 1 - Existing warm investor / angel contact

This is your highest-yield channel. Send this first, before any of the
others.

**Subject:** *Quick funding ask - AMASAMYA closed beta*

> Hi [first name],
>
> Hope you're well. I'm reaching out with a specific, small ask, not
> a pitch.
>
> Since [last time we spoke / your earlier interest in my work], I've
> finished AMASAMYA - a WCAG 2.2 audit platform for the web, documents,
> and mobile, designed blind-first. It's at amasamya.akhileshmalani.com,
> Chrome extension is in Web Store review, and the test suite + axe
> self-audit are clean.
>
> I'm raising **$[X]** to fund a closed beta of 8 paid screen-reader
> sessions, plus a 6-week build sprint to public launch. Use of funds,
> milestones, and reporting cadence are in the attached one-pager.
>
> Three ways you could help, in descending order of preference:
>
> 1. A small cheque ($[1k]–$[5k]) - I'll commit to monthly written
>    milestone updates for six months.
> 2. An introduction to one person in your network who'd care about
>    accessibility tooling.
> 3. 30 minutes on Zoom to tell me what's missing from my plan.
>
> No reply needed if none of these fit. If one does, I'll send the
> short due-diligence packet within 24 hours of your reply.
>
> Thank you for the time -
>
> Akhilesh Malani
> [link]

## Things every email should do

- ✅ Specify the dollar amount you're asking for.
- ✅ Itemise what the money funds (3–5 lines).
- ✅ Give a clear yes/no path and an opt-out path.
- ✅ Attach the one-pager. Don't make them ask.
- ✅ Sign off with a single name and a single link.

## Things to never do

- ❌ Use the word "synergy", "leverage", "scale", or "disrupt".
- ❌ Promise a 100x return.
- ❌ Use the phrase "I'm not asking for money" if you are asking for
  money. Say what you want.
- ❌ Send the same email to multiple recipients on a CC line.
- ❌ Hide the dollar number until "we can discuss it on a call."
- ❌ Follow up before 7 days. Don't follow up more than twice.

---

## Timing - when to actually send

Track 1 (warm contacts): any weekday morning 8–10 a.m. local. Avoid
Mondays (everyone's inbox is on fire) and Fridays after 2 p.m.
(everyone's checking out).

Track 2 (cold investors): same, but Tuesday/Wednesday only.

Track 3 (pilot customers): Tuesday 10 a.m. their time zone.

Track 4 (grant + accelerator): whenever the application opens. Don't
wait for a "good time" - they have firm deadlines.
