Visual Layout Auditor

Emulates four viewport widths (320px, 375px, 768px, 1280px), captures a screenshot at each breakpoint, and uses Vision AI to detect overlapping elements, clipped content, reflow failures, and small touch targets. Covers WCAG 1.4.10 (Reflow) and SC 2.5.5 (Target Size) visually.

Audit a whole website (Site Crawl)

This runs the AMASAMYA audit on many pages of a website, one after another, instead of just the page you are on. The results go to the AMASAMYA web platform if you have it open in another tab. Up to 200 pages per run.

Which pages do you want to check?

If you are not sure which option to pick, choose the first one and paste 3 or 4 page addresses to start.

Each line must start with https:// or http://. Up to 200 addresses. If you paste more, the extra ones are ignored with a notice before the audit starts.

AMASAMYA tries to read the website's published list of pages (called a sitemap). This works on most marketing and content sites. Large stores like Amazon and most banks block this on purpose; for those sites, switch to the first option above and paste the page addresses yourself.
